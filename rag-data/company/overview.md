# About Orbinex

Orbinex is an **enterprise-grade AI assistant platform** designed for the next generation of customer engagement. We empower companies to integrate AI seamlessly into their websites and products.

## Vision

To make advanced AI accessible to every business, from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

## Core Values

- **Innovation First** - Constantly pushing boundaries of what's possible
- **User Privacy** - Data stays secure and on-premise by default
- **Developer Friendly** - Simple, powerful APIs for quick integration
- **Enterprise Ready** - Security, scalability, and reliability built-in

## Key Features

- 🤖 **Advanced AI Conversations** - Claude-powered intelligence
- 🛠️ **Rich Tool Ecosystem** - Weather, currency, calculations, and more
- 📚 **RAG System** - Learn from your own documents and knowledge bases
- 🎨 **Premium UI** - Beautiful, modular chatbot widget
- 🔒 **Enterprise Security** - On-premise deployment options
- 📊 **Analytics** - Track conversations, engagement, and insights

## Team

Our team consists of AI researchers, full-stack engineers, and product designers passionate about making AI accessible.

## Timeline

- **Q1 2024**: Platform launch
- **Q2 2024**: RAG system release
- **Q3 2024**: Advanced analytics suite
- **Q4 2024**: Enterprise compliance (SOC 2, HIPAA)

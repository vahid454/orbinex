# Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I get started with Orbinex?
A: 
1. Sign up at https://orbinex.dev
2. Get your API keys
3. Add 1 line of code to your website
4. Start talking to your AI assistant!

### Q: What's the setup time?
A: Usually less than 5 minutes. Most of our users are live within the hour.

### Q: Do I need to know how to code?
A: The embed code is simple HTML/JS. No coding required, but customization is available for developers.

## Technical Questions

### Q: What AI model do you use?
A: We use Claude Haiku 4.5 (fast) or Claude Opus (advanced), both by Anthropic. You can choose based on your needs and budget.

### Q: Can I use it with my own LLM?
A: Yes! Enterprise customers can deploy with custom models. Contact sales@orbinex.dev

### Q: Is RAG included?
A: RAG is included with Professional and Enterprise plans. It lets your AI learn from your documents.

### Q: What data gets stored?
A: Only conversations (configurable). Your RAG documents never leave your server with on-premise deployment.

## Support & Security

### Q: How's my data protected?
A: We use industry-standard encryption, regular security audits, and optional on-premise deployment. Enterprise customers get SOC 2 compliance.

### Q: What's your support like?
A: Email support included with all plans. Professional+ includes priority support. Enterprise gets 24/7 dedicated support.

### Q: Do you have an SLA?
A: Yes! Professional: 99% uptime. Enterprise: 99.9% uptime with credits for downtime.

## Pricing & Billing

### Q: Can I customize the pricing?
A: Yes! Enterprise plans are fully customized. Contact sales@orbinex.dev for a quote.

### Q: Is billing monthly or annual?
A: Both options available. Annual plans get 20% discount.

### Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Starter and Professional: Yes, anytime. Enterprise: Annual commitment with early termination fees.

### Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: 30-day money-back guarantee on Starter and Professional plans.
